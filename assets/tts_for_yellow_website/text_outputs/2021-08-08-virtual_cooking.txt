Virtual Cooking, by Emily Mackevicius, August 08, 2021: We had an impromptu chicken roast on Zenna's floor. Then the four of us played "Overcooked", a collaborative cooking video game on twitch. I'm too sleepy to convey quite how wonderful the gathering was, making me feel alive in ways I'd been dearly missing. I'll just ask you to appreciate the ruckus thrill of a fully in person virtual cooking game.
